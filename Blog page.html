What are the benefits of herbs

Natural herbs have many health benefits, They contain antioxidants such as polyphenols that help reduce inflammation and protect cells from damage. Some herbs such as mint and ginger also contribute to improving digestion and relieving nausea and vomiting, Herbs such as cloves and ginger can help relieve tooth and joint pain thanks to their anti-inflammatory properties.

What are herbal medicines

Herbal medicine is an important part of traditional medicine and natural therapy, and has been used for thousands of years to treat a variety of diseases and health conditions, These drugs are based on the extraction of active compounds from herbs and their use in various therapeutic formulations

The benefits of herbal products

Herbal products are considered healthy and natural alternatives to many traditional products, and are gaining increasing popularity thanks to their multiple benefits, These products rely on natural ingredients, which reduces the risk of exposure to harmful chemicals or side effects
